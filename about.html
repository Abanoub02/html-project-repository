How can I submit a site for consideration?


You can submit a site by clicking here.


I submitted a site but it hasn't appeared - why?


siteInspire receives over 200 subsmissions per day so
it unfortunately it is impossible to feature every site,
so we have to limit the number of posts

.

How do you decide which sites get featured?


We look for cleanliness and simplicity without excessve
gimmicks or decoration. Sites that use Flash unnecessarily
won't be featured (though appropriate Flash sites are not excluded).
Sites that force the browser's size will not be accepted. Sites with
broken images or obvious visual errors won't be featured (because we can't
take a nice screenshot). Also, sites that feature "award ribbons" are not
featured (because award ribbons are a quick way to make a site ugly).
Some of the sites you feature are really hard to use, or don't work in my browser

.

Why have you featured it?


Not every site featured is perfect and some might even contain errors, but we hope
all are interesting and push the boundaries of visual design and interaction.

Can I pay you to feature my site?


No - none of the features have been paid for.

Do you feature themes/templates?


Not yet but a new section will appear soon dedicated to themes and templates.
You can already choose to submit themes.
